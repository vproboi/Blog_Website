Places Must Visit

disney land

i had visited disney land,hong-kong in september 2018

My experience at Hong Kong Disneyland was truly magical. As soon as I entered the park, I was greeted by the iconic Sleeping Beauty Castle, which served as a beautiful centerpiece. The entire park was filled with vibrant colors and cheerful music, instantly transporting me into a world of enchantment. I started my day by exploring Main Street, U.S.A., a charming street lined with Victorian-style buildings and quaint shops. It felt like stepping back in time to a nostalgic era. I indulged in some delicious snacks and enjoyed browsing through the various stores filled with Disney merchandise. Next, I ventured into Adventureland, where I embarked on thrilling attractions like the Jungle River Cruise and the Rafts to Tarzan's Treehouse. The lush greenery and immersive theming made me feel like I was on a real jungle expedition. The highlight of Adventureland was definitely the "Festival of the Lion King" show, featuring incredible acrobatics, colorful costumes, and beloved characters from the film. I then made my way to Fantasyland, which was a fairy tale dream come true. I hopped on classic rides like "It's a Small World" and "Dumbo the Flying Elephant." The magical ambiance, adorned with beautiful castles and whimsical characters, truly brought out my inner child. I even met some of my favorite Disney princesses and took memorable photos with them. In Tomorrowland, I experienced futuristic attractions like "Buzz Lightyear Astro Blasters" and "Space Mountain." The adrenaline rush from these rides was exhilarating, and the immersive theming made me feel like I was soaring through space. The "Disney Paint the Night" parade in the evening was an absolute spectacle, with dazzling lights, vibrant floats, and beloved Disney characters lighting up the night sky. Throughout the day, I was impressed by the impeccable service and friendliness of the cast members. They went above and beyond to create a magical experience for every visitor. The attention to detail in every corner of the park was astounding, and it truly showcased the Disney commitment to creating unforgettable memories. As the day came to a close, I found myself captivated by the spectacular fireworks display above Sleeping Beauty Castle. The burst of colors and enchanting music left me in awe. It was a perfect finale to an extraordinary day at Hong Kong Disneyland. Overall, my experience at Hong Kong Disneyland was nothing short of enchanting. It was a place where dreams came true, and I left with cherished memories that will last a lifetime. I can't wait to visit again and relive the magic all over again.

mong kok

i had visited mong kok,hong-kong in september 2018

My experience on Hong Kong's Mong Kok Neon Street was absolutely mesmerizing. As soon as I arrived in the bustling district of Mong Kok, I was greeted by a vibrant kaleidoscope of neon lights that illuminated the entire street. The moment I stepped onto Neon Street, I was engulfed in a whirlwind of sights and sounds. The neon signs, in all their dazzling glory, adorned the facades of buildings and created a dazzling canopy of colors overhead. The energy of the street was electric, and I couldn't help but feel a sense of excitement and wonder. As I walked along the street, I was drawn to the eclectic mix of shops and market stalls that lined the sidewalks. It was a shopper's paradise, offering everything from trendy fashion boutiques to quirky novelty stores. The vibrant displays and enticing storefronts were impossible to resist, and I found myself getting lost in the excitement of exploring each unique shop. The street was also home to an array of street food stalls, each offering a tantalizing selection of local delicacies. The aroma of sizzling meats, steaming dumplings, and freshly brewed teas filled the air, enticing me to indulge in the culinary delights of Hong Kong. I savored every bite, immersing myself in the flavors and textures that made the local cuisine so remarkable. One of the highlights of my experience on Neon Street was observing the vibrant street performances that took place throughout the evening. Talented musicians, dancers, and artists showcased their skills, captivating passersby with their incredible talents. The lively atmosphere and the applause of the crowd created an electric ambiance that added to the overall charm of the street. As the night progressed, the neon lights of Mong Kok intensified, casting a magical glow over the entire area. The symphony of colors and the pulsating energy of the street created a surreal and unforgettable experience. I couldn't help but be captivated by the unique blend of modernity and tradition that Neon Street embodied. Leaving Mong Kok Neon Street was bittersweet, as I had become enamored with its vibrant charm. The memories I made and the sensory overload I experienced will forever be etched in my mind. It was a visual feast that awakened my senses and immersed me in the dynamic spirit of Hong Kong's street culture. Visiting Mong Kok Neon Street was truly an adventure for the senses. It was a place where tradition and modernity collided, creating a captivating tapestry of lights, sounds, and flavors. It is a destination that I would highly recommend to anyone seeking a unique and unforgettable experience in Hong Kong.

Review On The Movies

Mrs Chatterjee vs Norway
Excellent movie to depict mothers love

Nothing comes close to maternal love and the pain of a mother whose kids have been taken away from her forcefully on the pretext of improper parenting. The official list of charges against her include reasons like sleeping in the same bed, hand-feeding her kids and applying kohl on their faces to ward off evil eye. She is called mad, unstable and unfit to be a mother, but she will fight against all odds to get her kids back. That’s the ordeal Mrs Chatterjee Vs Norway takes us through. Based on a true story of Sagarika Chakraborty’s and adapted from her autobiography The Journey of a Mother, the film is a heart-wrenching tale of a couple whose both children were separated from them by the Norwegian Childcare system in 2011. Starring Rani Mukerji in the titular role as the shattered and helpless mother who stands tall against the system, this Ashima Chibber directorial is emotionally charged, albeit an unbalanced premise and flawed execution that can’t be overlooked completely. Pathaan
A great action movie with two khans

I went in to see this because of positive RT scores and comments which said this movie reeked of action. The reviewers just left out the part where they were supposed to say the action was unbelievable. It is worth a watch if you have 2.5 hours to kill. The fight choreography between SRK and 'Jim' (I'm too tired to look up the actor's name) were exceptional. Those featuring Padukone were kind of hit and miss. Sometime they reached Marvel's Black Widow heights, other times they were almost laughable.
